Community of the Week
Association for Computing Machinery
The Association for Computing Machinery is a US-based international learned society for computing. It was founded in 1947 and is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society.
Third place of the Week
Central Park
Central Park is centrally located in the city of Santa Clara, providing easy access for residents and visitors. The park is relatively spacious, featuring green spaces for outdoor activities.
